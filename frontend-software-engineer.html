Front-end Software Engineer (React.js)

Vilnius

We are an experienced and innovative technology team of more than 30 software development professionals who enjoy 10+ years of successful partnership with Nordic technology company Boozt.

Being the second biggest tech team of this huge international business with more than 500 colleagues of 30+ nationalities, we focus purposefully on building real big scale and impeccable quality solutions for e-commerce platform: from core backend, shop frontend and API to mobile apps.

We strongly believe this always allowed us to learn quickly, pay attention to the smallest details, stay streamlined, and close to the business, which got us where we are today.
