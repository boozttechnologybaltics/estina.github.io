Site Reliability Engineer

Vilnius, Lithuania

We are an experienced and innovative technology team of more than 30 software development professionals who enjoy 10+ years of successful partnership with Nordic technology company Boozt.

Being the second biggest tech team of this huge international business with more than 500 colleagues of 30+ nationalities, we focus purposefully on building real big scale and impeccable quality solutions.

Join our Platform team as a Site Reliability Engineer at Vilnius office, and be a part of building the most advanced technology platform for e-commerce. Our systems are built in close collaboration with the business specialists and based on open technologies such as PHP, Symfony, MySQL supported by hybrid infrastructure and managed with top of the shelf tools such as Ansible, Chef, NewRelic.
