How we roll

Being the second biggest tech team of a huge international company with more than 500 colleagues, here in the Vilnius office we are concentrated on delivering the best development solutions. Although we’ve been going through a fast growth during the past few years, we’ve managed to keep working in small teams of 4 developers. We strongly believe this always allowed us to learn quickly, pay attention to the smallest details, stay streamlined and close to the customers which got us where we are today.
